About

Why was this tool created?

This website was created to help people explore athletic finances at schools in the Mid-Atlantic Conference. The current way to access data isn't very helpful for people to understand the impact of financials, so this side-by-side comparison hopes to give insight on how much monentary impact sports programs have on universities.

Where was the data curitated from?

Some of the data was pulled from an USA Today Table, while others were collected from the NCAA website and the National Institution of Educational Statistics.

Will this tool be build upon for the future?

Yes. We are planning to show these schools expenses and revenues over time, the cost of each sports, as well as adding other conferences.
