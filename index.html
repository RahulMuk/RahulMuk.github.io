MOBILE MANIA

 

This infographic was created to show the growing trends of mobile phones for the past two decades. I added the distribution of OS to show how the mobile market is shifting more towards smartphones. I also added which age groups use the most phones, as well as the complexity of how a phone is built to show the great amount of care is used in making this device. The module on the bottom right was used to show that phones are used less for communication purposes and more for entertainment.

  • Client: Spark Magazine

Communication Breakdown

 

For this infographic, I wanted to show how fiber optic cables, which were being implemented in my hometown, would impact internet speeds. I made a map that would be able to show speeds across the country, and showed the five biggest speeds overall. Also, I wanted to show the cable in general, so I showed how data is transfered, the types of transmissions, types of cables, and the parts of it.

  • Client: Spark Magazine
