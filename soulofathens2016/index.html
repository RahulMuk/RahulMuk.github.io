The Soul of Athens multimedia project started in 2006 as a showcase that explored the characters and stories of Athens, Ohio; a small community in the foothills of Appalachia. Beginning in the spring of each year, student photographers, videographers, writers, and designers collaborate to publish a series of visual narratives. It began with the question: What makes Athens unique? Now celebrating the 10th anniversary of Soul, the 2016 installment has taken a new direction. The Soul of Athens team carries on the storytelling tradition beyond just Athens, Ohio but instead across the entire region to uncover why people call Appalachia home.
