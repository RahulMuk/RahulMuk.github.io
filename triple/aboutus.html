About Us

Cal Al-Dhubaib

CEO / CTO / Founder

Cal Al-Dhubaib founded Triple Analytics in May 2014 after three years research experience in Saudi Aramco's hospital system. He is a senior BA Computational Neuroscience and MS Systems Engineering candidate at Case Western Reserve University and a board member of HIMSS Northeast Ohio chapter. Since 2013, Cal has won two research awards and three business awards for his work in healthcare analytics. He leads product and customer development and makes sure everyone on the Triple Analytics team has the resources they need to succeed.

Chantel Moody

Business Development Specialist

Chantel Moody is a MSM-Healthcare Candidate at Case Western Reserve University. She has worked for several Fortune 500 companies in various sales and business development roles in the pharmaceutical and medical device industry. Chantel will be responsible for assisting with the formal development of the business and sales strategy, as well as our investor strategy campaign.
