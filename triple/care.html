Care Providers

Triple Analytics clinical software package is the first combined clinical decision support and electronic prior authorization platform. The platform is intended to give care providers more autonomy over their treatment selections, based on evidence-based data analytics supporting the best treatment for each patient.

Outcome Predictor

The Outcome Predictor, used at the point-of-care, is intended to supplement care providers’ decision-making protocols. This analytics platform turns clinical data into actionable insights to make treatment decisions with a greater chance of PA approval.

PriorAuth Portal

The provider-facing portal, when used in conjunction with the Outcome Predictor, will speed up the prior authorization process. Providers can submit and view PA’s in real-time, with strong evidence-based medical support found in existing clinical data.
