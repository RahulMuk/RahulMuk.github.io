Want to Take a Spin?

Whether you like your ales blonde or your lagers dark, you’ve found our site in pursuit of your two great loves: craft beer and music on vinyl. We get it—nothing can top the warm buttery notes of a scotch ale or Al Green’s “Let’s Stay Together” on vinyl LP. But whether the tunes go with the brew is another matter, and that’s where we come in. Give us a beer style, we’ll select the right album to listen while you drink. Or name an LP and we’ll suggest the right beer to pair it with. Or leave it all up to us and we’ll find the right music and beer to fit the mood you’re in. Welcome to Spin the Bottles. Now it’s your turn.
