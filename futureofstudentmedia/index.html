THE FUTURE OF STUDENT MEDIA

Innovate, Change, Thrive | April 8 and 9, 2016 | Athens, OH

The Conference

What is it?

Today, there are unprecedented challenges facing collegiate media. To address this, Ohio University and The Post, its independent student-run news outlet, are bringing student media innovators from across the country to Athens, OH to discuss the Future of Student Media.

Panels, speeches, presentations and sessions will focus on pressing college student and professional leaders of college media organizations to innovate and change.

Why Attend

Too often, collegiate media operate in a vacuum without the support of experienced people who can encourage, suggest and advise change. Ohio University and The Post are bringing innovators together for the collective good of collegeiate student media outlets at America's colleges and universities.

Summit attendees will also be given a report about college student media consumption and national college advertising trends paid for by Ohio University.

Student editors, business managers, media directors and faculty advisors should attend.

More Information

The summit will run April 8 and 9, 2016. It will be located on Ohio University's campus in Athens, OH.

More information about registration, lodging, speakers and a schedule will be released soon.
